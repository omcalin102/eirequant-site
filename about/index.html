About EireQuant

An open, living quant research lab—built in public.

Last updated: 2025-08-17

What we publish

  • Model families: grouped “series” of Marks (e.g., EQ Core Mk I–III, Sentinel).
  • Portfolios: allocation rules with equity, drawdown, and turnover metrics.
  • Compare: a “mega graph” to overlay benchmarks, models, and portfolios.
  • Reports & notes: dev logs, research write-ups, and methodology summaries.

How the site works

To keep the site fast and predictable, data lands as static JSON files under /public/data/**. Pages and charts read those files at runtime. That means new models or portfolios are as simple as: add an MD file in content/**, drop the JSON time-series, and update the registry—no servers required.
